Top CLJS tickets by weighted vote

Date: July 4, 2013

Open CLJS tickets with at least one vote, sorted in descending order of their weighted vote.  Suppose someone has currently voted on N open tickets.  Then their vote counts as 1/N for each of those tickets.  Thus voting on all tickets has the same relative effect on their ranking as voting on no tickets.  You must be selective to change the rankings.

Each person gets 1 weighted vote to divide up as they wish for each project, i.e. 1 for CLJ, 1 for CLJS, 1 for MATCH, etc.
