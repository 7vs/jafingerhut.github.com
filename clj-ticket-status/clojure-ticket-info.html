    • "In particular I use votes to decide what gets triaged.  We also used votes as a way to see which issues needed to be kept in [Clojure] 1.7 this week vs pushed off to 1.8.  So I'm telling you (as the one that makes those decisions), that it's useful. I look [at] those numbers every day." -- Alex Miller (puredanger) on #clojure IRC log Oct 8 2014
